My Biography

My name is Tarik Ajanović. I am 22 years old and I am from Sarajevo, Bosnia and Herzegovina. I am a senior student at the Faculty of Science of the University of Sarajevo. I chose computer science as my bachelors degree because I have been drawn to it since my childhood. My goal is to someday become a video game developer in a renowned studio or start my own game development company.
Besides computer science I like to read and play the guitar. In my freetime I like to play all kinds of sports. I am a long-distance runner and a blue belt in Japanese Jiu-Jitsu.
